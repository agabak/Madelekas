WELCOME TO MADELEKAS ADVOCATES

Headquartered at PPF PLAZA Building in Arusha-Tanzania, Madelekas Advocates was registered on the 19th Day of October 2016, as a Law Firm under the Business Names (Registration) Act [Cap.213). The Firm was founded by two partners namely; Jamila Ilomo and Peter Michael Madeleka, both being Advocates with excellent credentials. With their years of expertise in varied areas, the Partners offer practical, creative and innovative solutions to the Firm’s clients to suit their specific needs through a holistic approach.

FIRM PROFILE

The Firm offers a broad range of services including but not limited to, a comprehensive legal consulting service, dispute resolution, litigation, arbitration, tax, immigration, citizenship, foreign investment, mining, oil and gas, capital markets, employment, finance, company restructuring, conveyance, environment, intellectual property, insolvency, trusts and estates, with teams adept at handling complex multi-jurisdictional transactions. The Firm’s collective experience provides an incomparable level of insight and sensitivity towards the commercial objectives of clients to provide innovative and value-driven settlements. A comprehensive understanding of the global economy has led the Firm to represent high net-worth clientele in diverse sectors. The Firm is strongly committed to providing the finest services to its clients, with unwavering dedication and commitment to excellence. The comprehensive practice areas of the Firm, which combine litigation and corporate advisory, have enabled the Firm’s attorneys to provide business friendly, practical and sound advice. The Firm’s strength is largely attributable to the dedication and commitment of our high-calibre lawyers who help deliver on the promise to provide quality legal and client service. The Firm has developed the crucial technical and interpersonal skills, business acumen and commercial expediency required to offer the best services to the Firm’s clients worldwide. The Firm also goes beyond the boundaries of traditional advocacy, developing strong professional alliances with several international law firms to truly position it as a global law firm.
